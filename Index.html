About Me

Bonjour! I’m Emily, a Chicago girl who packed her bags and moved to Paris to follow my dreams (and my career!). By day, I’m a marketing executive bringing my American perspective to the chic world of Parisian luxury brands. By night (and weekends!), I’m a full-time adventurer, discovering everything the City of Lights has to offer—from hidden cafes in Le Marais to glamorous soirées in the 8th arrondissement.

I believe that life is meant to be lived boldly, whether that means taking a risk on a new career, embracing a foreign culture, or wearing a really fabulous pair of shoes. My blog is a space where I share my love for fashion, food, and the art of finding beauty in unexpected places. From style tips to travel guides, I hope to inspire you to live your most colorful and confident life, wherever you are.

Follow along for a behind-the-scenes look at my Parisian life, the highs, the challenges, and everything in between—always with a smile, a little bit of flair, and a croissant in hand! XOXO, Emily

MUST SEES

  • Eiffel Tower Kick off your day with a sunrise visit to the Eiffel Tower. It’s less crowded, and the light is perfect for photos. Don’t forget to grab a croissant on the way—breakfast with a view!
  • Louvre Museum A trip to Paris wouldn’t be complete without seeing the Mona Lisa up close. Pro tip: Take a selfie outside the iconic glass pyramid; it's a total Insta moment!
  • Pont Alexandre III This bridge is one of my favorite spots in Paris—so romantic, especially at sunset. It’s a great place for a stroll or a photo shoot. Plus, the backdrop is très magnifique!
  • Explore Montmartre Sacré-Cœur- Walk up the steps to this stunning basilica for the best panoramic views of Paris. La Maison Rose- A pink house that’s become a social media sensation. Snap a photo here before wandering the charming, hilly streets of Montmartre.
