About Me

Bonjour! I’m Emily, a Chicago girl who packed her bags and moved to Paris to follow my dreams (and my career!). By day, I’m a marketing executive bringing my American perspective to the chic world of Parisian luxury brands. By night (and weekends!), I’m a full-time adventurer, discovering everything the City of Lights has to offer—from hidden cafes in Le Marais to glamorous soirées in the 8th arrondissement.

I believe that life is meant to be lived boldly, whether that means taking a risk on a new career, embracing a foreign culture, or wearing a really fabulous pair of shoes. My blog is a space where I share my love for fashion, food, and the art of finding beauty in unexpected places. From style tips to travel guides, I hope to inspire you to live your most colorful and confident life, wherever you are.

Follow along for a behind-the-scenes look at my Parisian life, the highs, the challenges, and everything in between—always with a smile, a little bit of flair, and a croissant in hand! XOXO, Emily

MUST SEES

Instagram-worthy sights and must see monuments

Eiffel Tower

Kick off your day with a sunrise visit to the Eiffel Tower. It’s less crowded, and the light is perfect for photos. Don’t forget to grab a croissant on the way—breakfast with a view!

Louvre Museum

A trip to Paris wouldn't be complete without seeing the Mona Lisa up close. Pro tip: Take a selfie outside the iconic glass pyramid; it's a total Insta moment!

Pont Alexandre III

This bridge is one of my favorite spots in Paris—so romantic, especially at sunset. It’s a great place for a stroll or a photo shoot. Plus, the backdrop is très magnifique!

Montmartre

Sacré-Cœur- Walk up the steps to this stunning basilica for the best panoramic views of Paris. La Maison Rose- A pink house that’s become a social media sensation. Snap a photo here before wandering the charming, hilly streets of Montmartre.

SHOP PING

Fashion, Shopping, and Parisian Chic

Le Marais

A fashionista's paradise! Boutique shopping, vintage stores, and trendy cafes. My go-to shops are Sézane for Parisian-chic clothing and Merci for cool, quirky finds.

Paris La Table du Petit Déjeuner

If you're looking for luxury, head here. From Dior to Chanel, this street is the epitome of Parisian elegance. Even if you’re just window shopping, it’s a feast for the eyes.

Galeries Lafayette Haussmann

This historic department store is a true Parisian shopping mecca, featuring everything from high-end luxury brands to more affordable French labels. But the real showstopper is the stunning art nouveau glass dome and the rooftop terrace with panoramic views of Paris. Emily would spend hours here, browsing the latest fashion collections and taking in the breathtaking sights. You must visit The beauty section for French skincare and perfume, the designer floor for the latest trends, and the rooftop bar for a relaxing drink after a shopping spree.

AURA NTS

Spots to Dine Like Emily Cooper in Paris

Boot Café

Tucked away in Le Marais, this tiny coffee shop is a hidden gem. It's got great vibes and even better lattes. Plus, the blue storefront is très photogenic.

Avenue Montaigne

The go-to place for a refined and delicious brunch or lunch. The ambiance is warm and sophisticated, perfect for a mid-day break after exploring the nearby Louvre or Palais Royal. You must try the avocado toast with poached eggs, paired with one of their fresh-squeezed juices or a classic French quiche lorraine. Don't miss their freshly baked pastries if you're in the mood for something sweet!

Champs-Élysées

Treat yourself to macarons and tea in one of Paris's most iconic tea rooms. It’s the perfect spot to recharge after a morning of shopping and snap a few colorful photos.

Relais de l'Entrecôte

This place has a cult following for its steak-frites, served with their secret sauce. It's a no-reservation spot, so prepare to wait in line—but trust me, it's worth it!

Hemingway at the Ritz

End your night with a classy cocktail. It's pricey, but the atmosphere is unbeatable. Channel your inner Audrey Hepburn and soak in the glamour.
